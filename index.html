💡 Tips for Success
📱 Use Anki daily — free flashcard app that uses spaced repetition. Just 10 minutes a day makes a huge difference.
🗣️ Say it out loud — Bulgarian pronunciation is very consistent once you know the rules. Don't just read silently.
🎵 Watch/listen in Bulgarian — even 10 minutes of Bulgarian YouTube or music counts as practice.
🤝 Use it at home — try one Bulgarian phrase per day in real life with your training partner.
